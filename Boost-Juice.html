Boost Juice Bars is a global chain of beverage outlets specializing in fresh fruit juices, smoothies, and other light refreshments. Founded by Janine Allis in Australia in 2000, Boost rapidly rose to prominence in the juice and smoothie industry, boasting over 500 stores worldwide.

The primary offerings of Boost Juice Bars include fresh fruit juices, smoothies, and other nutritious beverages like protein shakes. In addition to these, Boost also serves light meals such as wraps, sandwiches, and salads.

MARKETING CAMPAIGN

Planning for marketing campaigns requires a strategic approach, focusing on creating compelling key visuals and identifying customer pain points to deliver a message. It involves understanding the target audience, crafting visually engaging content that highlights the solutions to their challenges, and consistently evaluating the campaign's effectiveness for necessary adjustments.

Tagline: "Ngày dễ dàng - Tràn năng lượng"

Date: From Jul to Aug in 2020

Pain Point:In the era of the Covid pandemic, people are facing numerous health and energy issues. In addition to the worry of avoiding the virus, they also need to ensure they have enough energy and health to overcome long, stressful days. The need for vitamin supplementation to boost immunity and keep the body healthy is increasingly high.

Key Message:With the slogan "Ngày dễ dàng - Tràn năng lượng", the message wants to convey that the product can help customers supplement necessary vitamins and nutrients. The product boosts the immune system, helps customers avoid the flu, and provides the necessary energy for them to get through the long day more easily.

Tageline: "Colour Blast - Bùng nổ sắc màu"

Date and time: Sep to Nov 2020

Pain Point:In everyday life, many people often feel tired and lack energy. At the same time, they also want to express their personalization and individuality in today's era.

Key Message: With the slogan "Colour Blast", the message we want to convey is that each glass of Boost Juice not only provides energy but also allows customers to express their individuality through their color choices. The product is not just a nutritious fruit juice but also a manifestation of creativity and distinct personality.

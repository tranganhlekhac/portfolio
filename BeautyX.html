MARKETING CAMPAIGN

Man

Tagline: "Tết là phải đẹp"

Date: From Jan to Mar in 2022

Pain Point: Increased beauty demand during Tet but facing risks at unreliable establishments, long waiting times, and non-transparent pricing.

Key message: "Not just beauty, but also safety - BeautyX brings a memorable Tet" emphasizing safety in beauty services during Tet holidays. This message ensures that customers not only meet the high demand for beauty during Tet but also guarantee the quality and safety of the services, along with transparent pricing by using BeautyX app.

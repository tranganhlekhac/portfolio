With Fresha, you can compare services, read reviews from other users, and book appointments 24/7 without the need to make phone calls or send emails.

MARKETING CAMPAIGN

Planning for marketing campaigns requires a strategic approach, focusing on creating compelling key visuals and identifying customer pain points to deliver a message. It involves understanding the target audience, crafting visually engaging content that highlights the solutions to their challenges, and consistently evaluating the campaign's effectiveness for necessary adjustments.

Tagline: BeautyX - Đặt lịch làm đẹp

D​ate: From Jun to Dec in 2022

Pain Point: In today's digital age, having to call or use social media channels to make appointments with spas can become inconvenient and time-consuming. Customers often have to wait for confirmation from staff and can easily fall into situations such as schedule conflicts, crowded spas, or encountering unreasonable deals.

Key Message: BeautyX offer customers a fresher and more convenient way to book appointments. Customers can easily schedule appointments and purchase attractive deals online, saving time and avoiding unwanted situations. BeautyX is not only an e-commerce platform, but also an application that helps enhance beauty in a safer, more efficient, & convenient way.

Tagline: "Tết là phải đẹp"

Date: From Jan to Mar in 2022

Pain Point: Increased beauty demand during Tet but facing risks at unreliable establishments, long waiting times, and non-transparent pricing.

Key message: "Not just beauty, but also safety - BeautyX brings a memorable Tet" emphasizing safety in beauty services during Tet holidays. This message ensures that customers not only meet the high demand for beauty during Tet but also guarantee the quality and safety of the services, along with transparent pricing by using BeautyX app.

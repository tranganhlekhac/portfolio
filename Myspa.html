Myspa

Software as a Service

Myspa, established in 2018, is an efficient and convenient management software designed specifically to meet the needs of spa, salon, clinic, and beauty center.... The software facilitates easy management of appointments, customer information, services, staff, and revenue, all within a simple and intuitive platform.

Tagline: "Số hóa khởi tạo thành công"

Date: From Oct 2021 to Mar in 2022

Pain Point: Many beauty business owners currently face a multitude of issues when using Excel or traditional methods to manage their businesses. Most beauty businesses have been burdened with operational costs and employee salaries. However, with Myspa's solution, these businesses can be better prepared to rebound and adapt to the new normal.

Key message: “Kinh doanh online - Vượt bão Covi” - Myspa offers flexible payment policies, allowing pre-payment and account activation only when the service is used. This helps businesses set up software in advance, so they are ready to operate once the situation improves. Additionally, Myspa supports omni-channel online sales for spas to sell their cosmetics or treatments online for future use. Navigate through the Covid storm and prepare for a stronger comeback with Myspa.

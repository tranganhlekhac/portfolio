Man

Tagline: "Tết là phải đẹp"

Date: From Jan to Mar in 2022

Pain Point: Increased beauty demand during Tet but facing risks at unreliable establishments, long waiting times, and non-transparent pricing.

Key message: "Not just beauty, but also safety - BeautyX brings a memorable Tet" emphasizing safety in beauty services during Tet holidays.
